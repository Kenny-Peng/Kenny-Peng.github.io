I detail and analyze my findings into the degradation of trust towards American institutions from polling data spanning nearly 100 years and summarize it in this paper "The Deterioration of American Institutional Trust". This paper focuses specifically on the emotion of trust and its history over time, and what has caused trust to change. This is a perfect display of my critical thinking and analytical skills by which I condensed information and statistics into meaningful conclusions derived from that data.
