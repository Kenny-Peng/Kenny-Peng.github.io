In this whitepaper, "Does Anonymity Still Exist Online?", that I co-authored while collaborating with a team of 3 others, I discuss the ramifications of fingerprinting and tracking online on the privacy of users, and I suggest ways to protect against these methods of data collection and profiling using my experience as a web developer that's familiar with this type of software. This piece serves as an excellent example of my technical skills and ability to provide solutions to difficult problems using my prior experience.
